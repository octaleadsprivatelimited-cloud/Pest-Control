ABOUT FAMOUS PEST CONTROL SERVICES

Famous Pest Control Services has been serving Hyderabad and Khammam for over 15 years, providing professional pest control solutions for residential and commercial properties. Our certified technicians use advanced equipment and eco-friendly treatments to eliminate pests safely and effectively. We are committed to protecting your family, property, and business from harmful pests while maintaining the highest standards of service excellence.


COMPREHENSIVE PEST CONTROL

We provide complete pest elimination services for all types of pests including termites, cockroaches, ants, mosquitoes, rodents, and bed bugs. Our one-time treatments are designed to eliminate existing infestations and prevent future problems.

REGULAR MAINTENANCE PROGRAMS

Our monthly maintenance programs ensure your property stays pest-free year-round. We provide scheduled inspections and preventive treatments to protect your investment and maintain a healthy environment.

24/7 EMERGENCY SERVICE

Pest problems don't wait for business hours. Our emergency response team is available 24/7 to handle urgent pest infestations in Hyderabad and Khammam. We provide rapid response and immediate treatment solutions.

AFFORDABLE & TRANSPARENT PRICING

We offer competitive pricing with no hidden fees. Our transparent pricing structure ensures you know exactly what you're paying for. We provide free inspections and detailed quotes for all our pest control services.
